Brillance is a clean, modern SaaS landing page template designed to showcase products with clarity and trust. 
The layout flows smoothly from hero to features, pricing, and FAQs, giving startups a polished foundation to present their product.

Key Sections

✨ Hero & CTA
A bold headline with sub-text and a clear call-to-action to capture attention right away.

📊 Product Features
Feature blocks with supporting visuals/screenshots to highlight scheduling, analytics, and collaboration.

🤝 Social Proof & Integrations
Logos, testimonials, and tool integrations that add trust and show compatibility.

💵 Pricing Plans
Simple, tiered pricing cards that help users compare and choose easily.

❓ FAQ & Footer
Answers to common questions and a clean footer to wrap up the page.
